Blue Blue Japan

Indigo is more than a dye at Blue Blue Japan – it is the lifeblood of the company, coursing through nearly everything they produce. They name says it all, with production entirely in Japan and just about all of it blue.

Launched in 1996 in Tokyo as the in-house brand of OKURA, the brand has grown slowly, amassing a cult-like following of indigo lovers worldwide.

KUON

KUON is designed by Shinichiro Ishibashi. KUON means “eternity,” “remote past" or "future,” and also “permanence”.

While appreciating the history and culture of vintage clothes and textiles, KUON always seeks for permanence, simplicity, and authenticity — to not be bound by traditional views. KUON seeks to challenge customary notions about vintage clothes and to revitalize them with new value.
